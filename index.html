Flow chart

Login Screen

This screen comes up the first time a user downloads the app. They can log in for extra content (such as favorites) or continue without an account.

Home Screen

The Home screen shows series a user may be interested in. If the user is logged in it would first show recommended series, otherwise it would show trending/new series first. Users can favorite or share the series on social networks.

Favorites Screen

Here the user can look at favorite series and episodes. The three dot drop down bar will allow users to share their selection on social networks or remove from favorites.

Shop Screen

The Shop screen is an easy way to find products related to series/episodes users have watched.


Episodes List Screen

On the episode screen a user can see the latest episode and other episodes. User can filter episodes by season or date. Users can share the series or favorite it.


Watch Episode Screen

On the Watch screen users watch the episode they previously selected. They can enlarge the video to fit full screen with the square icon in the bottom right. Users can favorite or share the episode they are watching. Products related to the series (along with an optional company logo) are displayed underneath. Series a user may be interested in are displayed too. A user can also view or comment on the episode they watched.
